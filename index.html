The event

Theme : L'ère de rien.

L'air de rien ou l'ère de rien?

L'air de rien, beaucoup de choses peuvent se produire. Des problèmes paraissants anodins dans notre société aux sujets qui sont peu mis en lumière via les médias, en passant par des histoires de personnes aux apparences étonnantes et aux parcours incroyables. Découvrez comment, l'air de rien, beaucoup d'idées sont d'une importance capitale dans un monde en constante évolution.

L'ère de rien, notre période contemporaine est-elle dénuée de sens et refléte-t-elle parfois le néant ? Des thèses nihilistes aux antithèses optimistes, en passant par des synthèses réalistes. Découvrez comment, l'ère de rien, peut devenir un postulat aux multiples facettes et différentes interprétations.

Paul Tasner

How I became an entrepreneur at 66

It's never too late to reinvent yourself. Paul Tasner founded his own start-up at age 66, pairing his idea for a business with his experience and passion. As he shares in this short, funny and inspirational talk, seniors are increasingly indulging their entrepreneurial instincts -- and seeing great success.

Christiane Amanpour has spent the past three decades interviewing business, cultural and political leaders who have shaped history. She says : "Unless we are all engaged as global citizens who appreciate the truth, then we are going to be wandering around -- to a potential catastrophe."

Michael Norton

How to buy happiness

Michael Norton shares fascinating research on how money can indeed buy happiness -- when you don't spend it on yourself. Listen carefully for surprising data on the many ways pro-social spending can benefit you, your work, and (of course) other people in the world.

Naomi McDougall Jones

What it's like to be a woman in Hollywood

What we see in movies matters: it affects our hobbies, our career choices, our emotions and even our identities. Right now, we don't see enough women on screen or behind the camera -- but waiting for Hollywood to grow a conscience isn't going to fix the problem. Join forces as she outlines her four-point plan for a total representation revolution.

Learn more

Elizabeth Wayne

We can hack our immune cells to fight cancer

After decades of research and billions spent in clinical trials, we still have a problem with cancer drug delivery. Chemotherapy kills cancer -- but it kills the rest of your body, too. Instead of using human design to fight cancer, why not use nature's? Wayne explains how her lab is creating nanoparticle treatments to target cancer cells without damaging healthy ones.

About TEDx

What is TEDx ?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx.

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxMinesTélécom, where x = independently organized TED event.

At our TEDxMinesTélécom event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
